Overview


When songwriters gig, they are often required to add covers to their setlist of originals. Currently, there are apps to create set lists for covers and there are apps for setlist of originals, but there are no apps that allow you to combine both originals and covers.


This app allows songwriters to create set lists that have their original material and import songs that are covers.
